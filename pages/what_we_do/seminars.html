HBC routinely provide seminars and workshops for current and new clients. Seminars are usually two to four hours in duration. Workshops may vary from half-day, topic-specific sessions to multiple day retreats. The topics shown below are often augmented with either responses to either specific questions (refer to Common Inquiries) or questions that arrise under HBC's standard Services.

In addition to HBC's Consultants, speakers and facilitators often include nationally recognized insurance/health care regulatory law attorneys, actuaries, managed care/direct provider specialists, HIPAA/FERPA consultants, risk managers, insurance billing/coding consultants, and other experts HBC routinely retains. HBC often often works collaboratively with our client's students, faculty, staff, program leadership, and and other key stakeholders to jointly conduct seminars and workshops.
